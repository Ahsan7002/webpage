10/27/25: First Ride with the Panigale V4 R

Finally took the beast out today. The Ducati Panigale V4 R, 998cc of raw fury and unmatched precision. The throttle response was instant, and the exhaust note? Pure symphony. Every corner felt like poetry in motion. I could literally feel the engineering perfection Ducati poured into this machine.

Top speed test is next on the list, but for now, it’s just me, the open road, and that deep red roar.

10/26/25: Garage Upgrade Day

Spent the whole afternoon setting up my new garage space for the Panigale. Installed new LED panels, mounted a Ducati flag, and brought in a paddock stand. The bike deserves nothing less than a throne, after all, the V4 R isn’t just a bike, it’s a statement.

Next week: installing an exhaust from Akrapovič and getting a carbon fiber tail kit. Can’t wait to see it gleam under those lights.
